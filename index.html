Difference between successful and unsuccessful people

Successful People

They Want Others To Succeed

Their success is dependent on the success of others.

They Embrace Change

They understand that change is constant and they move with the trends rather than get stuck at a spot.

They Talk About Ideas

They are focused on getting their creativity juices going.

They Take Responsibility For Their Failure

They are in charge of their success.

They Continuously Learn New Thing

Their desire for knowledge is unending.

They Give Compliments

They are grateful and show genuine appreciation.

Unsuccessful People

They Want Others To Fail

Their success is dependent on the failure of others

They Are Afraid Of Change

Their hypocritical approach always put them behind the time.

They Talk About People

They are aimless breed born just to talk about others rather then working up on themselves.

They Blame Others For Their Failure

They lack introspection and are always dependent on others for their glory.

They Think They Know It All

They are self-obsessed and adamant in nature.

They Criticize Without Any Valid Reason

They have a habit of putting their feet in everyone shoes without knowing the size.
